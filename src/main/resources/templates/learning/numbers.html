Numbers

Numerals are words to describe quantity, they are used in counting and calculations, as well as defining the order.

We can distinguish caridnal numerals and oridnal numerals.

Rules for cardinal numerals

In masculine gender before the noun the form uno shortens to un. In feminine gender the suffix -o changes to -a:

  • un caso
  • una lista

Numerals between 20 and 30 are created with a modified numeral of tens number (veinti) and a numeral from 1 to 9:

  • 21 - veintiuno
  • 26 - veintiseis

Numerals between 30 and 100 are created with a numeral of tens number and a numeral from 1 to 9 using the conjunction y:

  • 47 - cuarenta y siete
  • 55 - cincuenta y cinco

We put the conjunction y only between the tens numbers and the numerals 1-9:

  • 1745 - mil sietesientos cuarenta y cinco

Ordinal numerals

Basic numbers

Most important numerals from which also other numerals can be made are listed below:

  • 1 - primero
  • 2 - segundo
  • 3 - tercero
  • 4 - cuarto
  • 5 - quinto
  • 6 - sexto
  • 7 - séptimo
  • 8 - octavo
  • 9 - noveno
  • 10 - decimo

Rules for ordinal numerals

They can be placed after or before the noun (more common):

  • Es mi segundo móvil.

They relate to the number and gender of the noun:

  • Segunda noche
  • Cuarto participante

Numerals primero and tercero shorten to the forms primer and tercer if they are placed before a noun:

  • El tiene un tercer perro.
