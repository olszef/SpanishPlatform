Verbs

Verbs are words that show an action (to sing), occurrence (to develop), or state of being (to exist).

In Spanish we have the verbs occuring with 3 different suffixes: -AR, -ER, -IR. Each suffix has different rules of conjugation. Below you can see how to conjugate the regular verbs in present tense (presente de indicativo).

Conjugation I - AR

Suffixes for each person / number:

Singular

  • Yo: -o
  • Tú: -as
  • Él/Ella/Usted: -a

Plural

  • Nosotros/as: -amos
  • Vosotros/as: -áis
  • Ellos/Ellas/Ustedes: -an

Example - BAILAR:

Singular

  • Yo bailo
  • Tú bailas
  • Él baila

Plural

  • Nosotros bailamos
  • Vosotros bailáis
  • Ellos bailan
